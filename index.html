Rules

1. The computer will generate a secret word for you (the user) to guess.

2. An empty noose will displayed on screen, and the secret word's length will be revealed by dashes displayed on the screen.

3. You (the user) will have six opportunities to guess the secret word. For each guess, you are only allowed to guess one letter out of the 26 letters in the English alphabet.

4. If you guess a letter and it is not in the secret word, one of the Hangman's body parts is attached to the noose on screen. You are NOT allowed to guess the same letter; if you do, then you will be alerted to guess a different letter. If you guess a letter and it is in the secret word, then it will be revealed where exactly in the secret word the letter holds a place.

5. If you guess the secret word correctly before all of the Hangman's body parts are attached to the noose, then you win! If not, then you lose, but you can always try again with the CPU generating a different word!

6. Good luck and have fun!
